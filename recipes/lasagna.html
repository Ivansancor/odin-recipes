Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a fantastic versatile dish with which to surprise guests of all ages!

Ingredients you will need:

  • Lasagna layers
  • Zucchini
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps to follow:

  1. Boil the lasagna
  2. Place the lasagna squares into an oven plate
  3. Cover with tomato sauce
  4. Cut the zucchini
  5. Place the zucchini on the tomato sauce
  6. Repeat the process until you're out of lasagna
  7. Sprinkle all the cheese on the top layer
  8. Put into the oven and cook at 180C for 45min
  9. ENJOY!
