Spaghetti Recipe

Spaghetti is a fantastic versatile dish with which to surprise guests of all ages!

Ingredients you will need:

  • Spaghetti
  • Zucchini
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps to follow:

  1. Boil the spaghetti
  2. Cover with tomato sauce
  3. Cut the zucchini
  4. Sautee the zucchini
  5. Add the zucchini
  6. Sprinkle all the cheese on the top layer
  7. ENJOY!
