Meatballs Recipe

Meatballs is a fantastic versatile dish with which to surprise guests of all ages!

Ingredients you will need:

  • Meat
  • Zucchini
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps to follow:

  1. Make the meat into balls
  2. Roll over egg and flour
  3. Cook in medium heat until cooked
  4. Cut the zucchini
  5. Mix in the zucchini
  6. Add tomato sauce
  7. Sprinkle all the cheese on the top layer
  8. ENJOY!
